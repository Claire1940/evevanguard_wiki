<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Add Non-Steam Game">
    Locate your local files and add the following executable to your Steam Library as a non-Steam game: `/path/to/steam/steamapps/compatdata/8500/pfx/drive_c/CCP/EVE/eve-vanguard/live/WindowsClient/start_protected_game.exe`.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Configure Compatibility">
    Right-click the newly created shortcut, navigate to **Properties**, head to **Compatibility**, and force the use of **GE-Proton10-9** or a newer version.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Extract Session Token">
    Launch EVE Online and start EVE Vanguard from the standard launcher. While the login screen is loading, open your terminal and run `ps aux | grep vanguard` to locate the active shipping client.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply Launch Options">
    Copy all parameters after `EVEVanguardClient-Win64-Shipping.exe`. Return to your Steam shortcut's **Properties** and paste this string into the **Launch Options**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Token Expiration Warning">
These session tokens are temporary. If your game fails to authenticate or prompts you to log in repeatedly, you must repeat the token extraction process to refresh your launch options.
</Callout>
