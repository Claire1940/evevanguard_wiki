<Section icon={Map} title="The Extraction Gameplay Loop">

The core of **eve vanguard pc** is the extraction-adventure loop. You deploy as an immortal Warclone into a hostile environment, complete objectives, and must reach an extraction point to secure your loot. If you die before extracting, you lose the resources gathered during that specific deployment.

**Phase 1: Deployment**
You drop into the battlefield with your chosen loadout. In Operation Avalon, this is typically the Lost Convoy map, characterized by crashed ships and industrial outposts.

**Phase 2: Objectives and Contracts**
Use your map to locate high-stakes contracts. These might involve hacking terminals, salvaging exotic technology, or eliminating specific Mordu's Legion commanders.

**Phase 3: Looting and Survival**
Search wreckage for Resonant Cyphers and advanced tech. Be wary of other Warclones; while you can cooperate, the scarcity of extraction points often leads to betrayal.

**Phase 4: Extraction**
Extraction points are not always active. You must monitor the battlefield timer and move to the designated zones when they open. The longer you stay, the more "Nemesis" threats will escalate, making survival significantly harder.

| Objective Type | Reward Tier | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Resource Salvage** | Low to Medium | Low - Common in outskirts. |
| **Data Hacking** | High | Medium - Requires time to stay stationary. |
| **Command Block** | Very High | Extreme - Located in the heart of the map. |
| **Bounty Hunting** | Variable | High - Involves PvP or Elite PvE. |

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Advice">
The "Command Block" at the center of the Avalon map is a high-risk zone. Only attempt this if you are in a squad with optimized gear, as it attracts the deadliest AI and most aggressive players.
</Callout>

One of the most unique features of **eve vanguard pc** is its direct connection to the EVE Online universe. Ground operations are not isolated; they contribute to a shared war effort known as World Events.

When Warclones successfully complete expeditions, they generate Resonant Cyphers and other data. This activity can trigger specific "AEGIS squalls" in EVE Online, creating new combat and hacking sites for space-faring capsuleers. This creates a symbiotic relationship where ground players and space players can support each other's progression.

| Reward Category | Vanguard Benefit | EVE Online Benefit |
