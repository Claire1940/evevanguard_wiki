<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategies for Operation Avalon">

Combat in EVE Vanguard is tactical and punishing. Because **eve vanguard free to play** access periods like Operation Avalon bring in many new players, understanding the meta-game is crucial for survival. You are fighting both AI-controlled "hordes of enemies" and other human Warclones who are after the same loot.

One of the unique features of Vanguard is the "Warclone" mechanic. You are essentially an immortal consciousness jumping between bodies. However, each body—and the gear it carries—is expensive. Dying means losing what you brought in, making every trigger pull a calculated risk.

**Tactical Recommendations:**
- **Controlled Bursts**: Recoil is significant; avoid "spraying and praying" at mid-range.
- **Sound Cues**: Listen for the heavy footsteps of other Warclones; stealth is often better than a direct confrontation.
- **Extraction Timing**: Don't wait until the last minute to find an extraction point. High-tier zones often have campers near the exit.
