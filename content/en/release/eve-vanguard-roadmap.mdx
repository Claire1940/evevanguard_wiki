<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Integration with EVE Online">

One of the most unique aspects of the **eve vanguard roadmap** is the "One Universe, One War" philosophy. Actions taken by Warclones on the ground directly impact the political and military landscape of EVE Online. This cross-game integration ensures that FPS players are not just playing in a vacuum but are active participants in the 20-year history of New Eden.

During Operation Avalon, Warclones can trigger "AEGIS Squalls" in EVE Online space by completing specific planetary objectives. These squalls create lucrative combat and hacking sites for capsuleers (space pilots), fostering a symbiotic relationship between the two player bases.

| Cross-Game Element | Impact on EVE Online | Impact on EVE Vanguard |
