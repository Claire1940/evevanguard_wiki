### Weapon Chipsets and Modifiers

Chipsets are advanced customization parts recovered from the battlefield or purchased through contracts. They allow Warclones to alter weapon characteristics permanently between deployments.

| Chipset Category | Primary Function | Ideal Tactical Scenario |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Damage Converters** | Switch ammunition profiles (e.g., Energy/Kinetic) | Stripping shields with energy before delivering kinetic finishing blows |
| **Fire Rate Modulators** | Adjust configurations between burst, semi-auto, and full-auto | Adapting a single rifle for both tight facility fights and open fields |
| **Recoil Stabilizers** | Drastically reduce vertical kick at the cost of handling speed | Long-range defensive setups when holding high-ground extraction points |

<Callout type="success" title="Loot Optimization">
Always prioritize extracting with rare weapon chipsets over basic industrial resources. Chipsets directly impact your combat efficiency in future deployments, whereas raw resources are primarily used for basic upkeep.

To ensure smooth gameplay during the **EVE Vanguard release** and current playtest phases, your PC must meet specific hardware standards. The game utilizes advanced modern rendering techniques, requiring a solid-state drive (SSD) and a DirectX 12 compatible graphics card to prevent performance bottlenecks.

| **Storage** | 50 GB available space (SSD required) | 50 GB available space (NVMe SSD recommended) |
| **DirectX** | Version 12 (DirectX 12.1 compatible) | Version 12 (DirectX 12.1 compatible) |
| **Target Performance** | 1080p, 60 FPS at Low settings (with FSR/DLSS) | 1080p, 60+ FPS at High/Ultra settings |

### Anti-Cheat and Compatibility Notes

- **Kernel-Level Anti-Cheat**: EVE Vanguard utilizes kernel-level **Easy Anti-Cheat (EAC)** to maintain fair play in its competitive PvPvE environments.
- **Steam Deck & Linux**: Due to the kernel-level anti-cheat requirements, Steam Deck and Linux compatibility is currently limited and may require custom Proton configurations.
- **Language Support**: The Steam client currently lists English as the primary supported language for interface and subtitles, though expanded regional localizations are planned for the full launch.
