<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Mechanics: Extraction and Combat">

EVE Vanguard is not a traditional arena shooter; it is an **extraction-adventure FPS**. Every deployment is a calculated risk where you play as an immortal Warclone. Your goal is to drop onto a planet, complete high-stakes contracts, salvage resources, and extract before the "Nemesis" threats or rival players eliminate you. If you fail to extract, you lose the gear and loot collected during that specific run.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="The Connection to EVE Online">

One of the most unique aspects of EVE Vanguard is its deep integration with the EVE Online universe. Actions taken on the ground by Warclones directly impact the "space war" handled by Capsuleers. This creates a "One Universe, One War" ecosystem where ground combat is not just a side-mode, but a vital part of the New Eden conflict.

**Key Connection Points:**
- **Resonant Cyphers**: Items recovered in Vanguard can be traded in EVE Online for SKINs and Skill Points.
- **AEGIS Squalls**: Successful ground expeditions can trigger events and combat sites in space.
- **Empire Support**: By trading resources in specific empire space (like Amarr or Caldari), players influence which faction gains a strategic advantage.
