<Section icon={Swords} title="Beginner Guide: Your First Deployment">

Starting your journey in the **eve vanguard open beta** requires a shift in mindset from traditional shooters. As an immortal Warclone, death is not the end, but losing your gear can set back your progression significantly. The gameplay loop follows a strict "Deploy, Loot, Fight, Extract" cycle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare at the Warbarge">
    Before launching, visit your Warbarge to customize your loadout. Choose your primary weapon and equip **Chipsets** that modify fire modes or damage types (Energy vs. Kinetic).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Planetary Descent">
    Drop into the Lost Convoy battlefield. Your first priority should be identifying the nearest extraction point and checking your active contracts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scavenge and Combat">
    Search crash sites and corporate installations for advanced technology and resources. Engage Mordu's Legion AI or rival Warclones only when necessary to secure high-value loot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Command Block">
    If you are feeling bold, push toward the center of the map to interact with the Command Block. This is a high-risk objective tied to the Nemesis storyline that yields the best rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Timed Extraction">
    Extraction points are not permanent. Watch the timer and the battlefield status. If you miss the extraction window, you lose all un-extracted resources gathered during the run.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Survival Strategy">
Always carry a **Nova Blade**. While firearms are your primary tool, the Nova Blade is essential for silent takedowns and conserving ammo when dealing with stray AI units.
</Callout>

</Section>
