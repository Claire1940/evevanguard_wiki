<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Craft and Upgrade Your Arsenal">

Fabrication is the cornerstone of progression in EVE Vanguard. You do not simply buy the best weapons from a market; you must earn them by gathering resources from fallen enemies, crashed warships, and planetary containers. Upgrading your weapons improves stability, increases magazine capacity, and unlocks specialized ammo types that bypass enemy shield configurations.

The crafting loop requires you to balance risk and reward. Venturing deeper into high-threat zones yields higher-quality materials, but increases the likelihood of encountering hostile warclones. Blueprints secured during deployments must be brought back to your orbital fabricator to unlock permanent crafting options.
