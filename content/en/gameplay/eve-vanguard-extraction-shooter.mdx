<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide: Deploying, Looting, and Extracting">

To successfully complete a deployment and secure your hard-earned loot, you must execute a methodical gameplay loop. Follow these tactical steps during your planetside missions:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Planetside Deployment">
    Spawn into the Lost Convoy battlefield. Immediately check your map for active contract objectives, nearby crash sites, and the locations of the Harmonic Bridges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire High-Stakes Contracts">
    Accept contracts to guide your run. These objectives will push you toward dangerous corporate-controlled installations, wreckage sites, or enemy strongholds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Raid and Salvage">
    Search Upwell salvage sites and crash zones. Open containers to reveal advanced technology and relics. Remember, opening containers triggers a short waiting period where loot is revealed one item at a time, leaving you highly vulnerable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat Mordu's Legion">
    Clear out elite ground troops guarding the best loot. Mid-way through, look for the Mordu's Legion Reserve Leader (marked by a red insignia) to secure the keycode for the high-yield Fueling Station tower.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Locate and Activate Harmonic Bridges">
    Locate an active Harmonic Bridge. Throughout the match, orbital bombardments will take these extraction sites offline one by one. Avoid the bombardments, find an active bridge, and defend it while it powers up.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Extract with Your Loot">
    Step into the activated Harmonic Bridge. Your warclone's organic body will burn up, but your consciousness and all secured loot will transport safely back to the orbiting Warbarge.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Extraction Tip">
As the match progresses, the environment becomes increasingly hostile and extraction sites disappear. If you wait until only a single Harmonic Bridge remains, expect intense PvP combat as rival warclones fight over the final exit.
</Callout>

</Section>
