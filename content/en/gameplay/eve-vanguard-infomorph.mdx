<Section icon={Shield} title="Understanding the EVE Vanguard Infomorph System">

In the dark sci-fi universe of New Eden, death is merely a temporary setback. As an immortal warclone, your consciousness is bound to an **eve vanguard infomorph**—a digital soul that can be projected across planetary battlefields. When you deploy planetside, you inhabit a heavily armed physical shell. If that shell is destroyed by hostile forces or rival players, your infomorph instantly uploads back to your orbital Warbarge, ready to inhabit a fresh clone.

This technology forms the foundation of the game's extraction-adventure loop. While your physical gear can be lost upon a failed extraction, your infomorph's memories, skills, and core progression remain intact. This system allows players to take extreme risks in high-stakes drop zones, knowing their immortality is guaranteed by advanced cloning technology.

| Mechanic | Description | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Infomorph** | Digital consciousness | Retains XP, unlocked blueprints, and core stats |
| **Warclone** | Physical cloned body | Lost upon death along with all unextracted loot |
| **Warbarge** | Orbital operations base | Used to manage loadouts and prepare next drops |

<Callout type="tip" title="Survival Tip">
Always prioritize extracting rare technology over securing extra kills. Your warclone body is highly replaceable, but the gear you carry is not.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Upgrading Your Warclone Attributes">

While your **eve vanguard infomorph** maintains your progression, you must actively upgrade your physical warclone attributes to survive deeper drops. Upgrades are divided into three main categories: survivability, weapon handling, and utility.

| Attribute | Focus Area | Upgrade Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shield Capacity** | Energy shield strength | Medium Nanites, Silicon Alloys |
| **Kinetic Armor** | Physical damage reduction | Heavy Plates, Carbon Fibers |
| **Stamina Recovery** | Sprinting and melee duration | Bio-reagents, Synthetic Enzymes |
| **Weapon Handling** | Recoil control and reload speed | Weapon Parts, Precision Calibrators |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Blueprints">
    Locate hidden data caches in corporate installations or complete high-stakes contracts to unlock upgrade blueprints.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Extract Required Components">
    Search crash sites and defeat elite ground troops to salvage the specific raw materials needed for your upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Upgrades at the Warbarge">
    Access the cloning bay on your Warbarge to permanently apply the upgrades to your future warclone deployments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Tip">
Focus on upgrading your Shield Capacity first. Energy shields regenerate automatically over time, providing the most reliable defense during chaotic PvPvE firefights.
</Callout>
