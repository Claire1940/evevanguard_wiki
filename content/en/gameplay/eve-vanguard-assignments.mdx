<Section icon={Map} title="Operation Avalon: Mission Structure and Tactics">

Operation Avalon represents a pivotal shift in how **eve vanguard assignments** are executed. Running from July 7 to July 20, 2026, this Alpha playtest focuses on "risk-based deployments." Unlike traditional shooters where the goal is simply to win a match, Vanguard requires you to balance your greed against the reality of the extraction timer.

The battlefield is a persistent zone where multiple Warclones may be pursuing the same or conflicting assignments. This creates a PvPvE environment where the greatest threat to your mission completion might not be the AI-controlled Mordu's Legion, but a rival player looking to intercept your loot.

**Key Mission Features in Operation Avalon:**
- **The Lost Convoy:** A massive, debris-strewn battlefield that serves as the primary zone for high-tier assignments.
- **Dynamic Extraction:** Extraction points are no longer static; they appear and disappear based on the progression of the session.
- **The Command Block:** A central objective tied to the Nemesis storyline that offers the highest rewards but attracts the most dangerous threats.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Assignment Completion Guide">

Completing **eve vanguard assignments** requires a disciplined approach. Rushing into an objective without a plan is a guaranteed way to lose your gear and your progress. Follow this tactical workflow to maximize your efficiency during the 2026 playtest.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Warbarge Preparation">
    Before launching, visit the Vanguard tab in the EVE Launcher. Review the available contracts and select one that aligns with your current gear. Ensure your weapon chipsets are configured for the damage types (Energy or Kinetic) most effective against the expected enemy forces.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Planetary Deployment">
    Upon landing, immediately check your tactical map. Identify the nearest objective point and locate potential extraction zones. Do not head straight for the center of the map; instead, move along the periphery to gather initial resources and scout for rival Warclones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Objective Engagement">
    When you reach the assignment location, clear the immediate area of Mordu's Legion forces. If the assignment involves hacking, set up defensive perimeters. Use the Nova Blade for silent takedowns to avoid alerting nearby squads of your presence.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resource Consolidation">
    Once the objective is complete, loot the area thoroughly. In Operation Avalon, wreckage and installations contain "exotic technology" that is essential for long-term progression. Prioritize high-value items like Resonant Cyphers over common materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Tactical Extraction">
    As soon as your primary objective is met, evaluate your health and ammo. If you are low on supplies, head for the nearest extraction point. Remember, an unextracted assignment provides zero rewards. Secure your loot and return to the Warbarge.
  </Step>
</Steps>
