<Section icon={Crosshair} title="Damage Types: Kinetic vs. Energy">

Understanding the duality of **eve vanguard weapons** damage is the difference between a successful extraction and a failed deployment. The 2026 combat model emphasizes a "Shield-then-Armor" approach. Most advanced enemies, including rival Warclones and elite Mordu's Legion troops, utilize energy shields that are highly resistant to physical projectiles but vulnerable to thermal and electromagnetic energy.

**Energy Damage Characteristics:**
- **Pros**: Rapidly depletes blue shield bars; high accuracy over distance.
- **Cons**: High heat generation; minimal impact on physical armor plates.
- **Primary Sources**: Laser DMR, Beam Rifle, Energy-converted SMG.

**Kinetic Damage Characteristics:**
- **Pros**: Shatters yellow armor plates; causes significant stagger.
- **Cons**: Heavily mitigated by active shielding; significant bullet drop.
- **Primary Sources**: Shrapnel Cannon, Standard SMG, Kinetic-converted Rifles.

| Defense Type | Weakness | Resistance | Visual Indicator |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Energy Shield** | Energy/Thermal | Kinetic/Impact | Glowing blue hexagonal mesh |
| **Physical Armor** | Kinetic/Impact | Energy/Thermal | Matte grey or metallic plating |
| **Structure** | High-Burst Kinetic | Sustained Energy | Exposed machinery/wiring |
