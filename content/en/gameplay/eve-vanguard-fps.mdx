<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Deployment Walkthrough">

Every deployment in EVE Vanguard is a calculated risk. You drop onto a hostile planetary surface with a set loadout, and your primary goal is to secure high-value salvage, complete contracts, and extract before the planetary defenses hunt you down.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare at the Warbarge">
    Before launching, return to your Warbarge—a personal mobile command space. Here, you will craft specialized equipment, purchase ammunition, and configure your modular weapon chipsets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Drop Planetside & Select Contracts">
    Upon landing in the combat zone, open your tactical map. Choose your objectives carefully: prioritize raiding crash sites, assaulting corporate-controlled forward bases, or hunting high-value targets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Fight and Hax">
    Engage PvE threats like Mordu's Legion or roaming Nemesis Drones. High-value resources are often locked behind secure containers; clear the area, initiate hacking protocols, and defend the site under pressure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Escalation Level">
    The longer you stay in a session, the higher the planetary alarm level rises. Hostile reinforcements will arrive in coordinated waves, culminating in deadly, chaingun-armed Oppressors dropping from the sky.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Initiate Extraction">
    Locate active extraction zones on your map. In Operation Avalon, extraction points are dynamic and contested. Coordinate with your squad, activate the extraction beacon, and defend the zone until your transport arrives.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Survival Rule">
If you die before successful extraction, your physical body is destroyed. While your consciousness is safely transferred to a new warclone body back on the Warbarge, you will **lose all unextracted loot and resources** carried in your inventory.
</Callout>
