<Section icon={Target} title="How to Extract: Step-by-Step Guide">

Extraction is the most dangerous part of any mission. In EVE Vanguard, the extraction process is not a simple "exit" button. It is a loud, visible event that signals your intent to every rival Warclone in the vicinity. During the Operation Avalon playtest (July 7–20, 2026), the extraction mechanics have been redesigned to emphasize contested zones and limited windows of opportunity.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate an Active Extraction Point">
    Open your tactical map to identify available extraction zones. Note that some points may be inactive or have limited capacity. In the Lost Convoy battlefield, these points are often centered around high-traffic areas.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Signal for Retrieval">
    Once you reach the extraction zone, you must initiate the signal. This often involves a countdown timer. Be aware that this action may alert nearby Mordu's Legion forces or rival players to your exact location.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defend the Perimeter">
    The extraction window takes time to open. You must hold your ground against escalating waves of enemies. Use the environment for cover and deploy any defensive gadgets or utility items you have scavenged.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure the Payload">
    When the extraction vehicle or portal arrives, you must physically enter the zone. Ensure all teammates are within the radius if playing in a squad. Once the timer hits zero, you are pulled back to orbit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
If you die before the extraction timer completes, you lose everything you collected during that run, including your current gear if it wasn't insured or part of a permanent loadout.
</Callout>

| Extraction Phase | Danger Level | Primary Threat | Recommended Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Approach** | Low | Snipers/Ambushers | Move between cover, use scans |
| **Signaling** | Medium | Mordu's Legion | Deploy proximity mines/turrets |
| **Holding** | High | Rival Warclones | Focus on 360-degree awareness |
| **Final Boarding** | Extreme | Grenades/Melee | Use smoke or suppression fire |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Looting Strategy and Resource Priority">

To make the most of this **eve vanguard extraction guide**, you must know what is worth carrying. Inventory space is limited, and every kilogram of loot slows you down or takes the place of something more valuable. The resources you find planetside are used to craft advanced weapon chipsets, upgrade your suit, and fuel the war effort in New Eden.

| Loot Category | Rarity | Primary Use | Where to Find |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tech Relics** | Rare | High-tier crafting | Corporate installations/Vaults |
| **Weapon Chips** | Medium | Modular gun upgrades | Enemy drop pods/Crates |
| **Raw Minerals** | Common | Basic ammo/Repairs | Surface deposits/Salvage sites |
| **Resonant Cyphers** | Event | EVE Online rewards | Operation Avalon event sites |

The introduction of the Command Block in recent updates has changed loot dynamics. This high-risk destination at the heart of the map offers the most lucrative rewards but is guarded by elite Mordu's Legion troops. Only prepared Warclones should attempt to raid this area.

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Tip">
If your inventory is full and you find a Tech Relic, drop your basic minerals. Relics provide significantly more progression value per slot than common resources.
</Callout>
