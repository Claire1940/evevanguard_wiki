<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Gameplay and Extraction Mechanics">

In this **EVE Vanguard tutorial**, understanding the flow of a match is critical. Unlike traditional arena shooters, Vanguard is about risk management. Every piece of loot you find is lost if you fail to extract. You will face both AI-controlled NPCs and other player squads (PvPvE), making every encounter a potential turning point for your mission.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Deployment & Infiltration">
    Select your loadout and drop into the planetary zone. Your initial priority is to scout the immediate area for resources while staying low to avoid early detection by Nemesis Drones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Objective Completion">
    Navigate to marked waypoints to raid crash sites or assault forward bases. These locations hold the exotic tech required to progress your standing and shape the larger interstellar war.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Extraction Phase">
    Once objectives are met, head to the extraction zone. This is the most dangerous part of the tutorial, as rival squads often camp these areas to steal your hard-earned loot.
  </Step>
</Steps>
