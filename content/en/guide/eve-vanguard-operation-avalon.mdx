Between expeditions, warclones return to their Warbarge. This serves as a persistent hub where you can craft new equipment using resources extracted from the planet. Progression is tied directly to your success in the field; if you fail to extract, you lose the gear you brought in, forcing a "zero-to-hero" run or the use of backup equipment stored in your hangar.

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Extraction Loop: A Step-by-Step Guide">

Success in **eve vanguard operation avalon** isn't measured by kills, but by what you bring home. The extraction loop is the heartbeat of the game, requiring a balance between greed and survival. Every minute spent on the surface increases the risk of being hunted by rival warclones or overwhelmed by escalating planetary defenses.
