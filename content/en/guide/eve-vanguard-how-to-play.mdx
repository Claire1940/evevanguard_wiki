Starting your first match can be overwhelming. Follow these steps to ensure a successful deployment and extraction during the playtest.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare on the Warbarge">
    Before launching, access your Warbarge to customize your starting loadout. Equip your primary weapons, select your starting chipsets, and ensure you have sufficient ammunition and healing supplies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy Planetside">
    Launch your drop pod to descend into the active combat zone, such as the Lost Convoy battlefield. Upon landing, check your map immediately to identify nearby contracts, hostile bases, and extraction points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Contracts and Salvage">
    Locate high-stakes contracts to earn credits and faction standing. Raid crash sites, defeat Mordu's Legion forces, and salvage advanced technology from wreckage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate to Extraction">
    Keep a close eye on the match timer and active extraction zones. When extraction points open, fight your way to the coordinates, call for retrieval, and defend the zone until you are safely extracted.
  </Step>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How Actions Connect to EVE Online">

EVE Vanguard is not just a standalone shooter; it is directly connected to the massive space MMO, EVE Online. Your actions on the ground influence the broader war in New Eden.

- **Shared World Events**: Completing ground expeditions can trigger environmental squalls and reveal hidden combat sites in EVE Online.
- **Resonant Cyphers**: Retrieve these special items during your deployments and trade them in EVE Online for rare blueprints, implants, and character customization options.
- **Factions War**: Your success or failure in planetary contracts directly supports or hinders the secret agendas of the major New Eden empires.

| Ground Activity | Space Impact | Capsuleer Reward |
|---|---|---|
| Complete Contracts | Triggers AEGIS squalls | Global Points |
| Salvage Technology | Spawns space convoys | Resonant Cyphers |
| Defeat Rival Clones | Alters faction influence | Faction Standing |

<Callout type="warning" title="Empire Standing">
Exchanging Resonant Cyphers in a specific empire's space strengthens their relationship with the Vanguard, altering where future playtests and story events take refuge.
</Callout>
