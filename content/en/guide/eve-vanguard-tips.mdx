<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Survival and Progression Guide">

To establish a successful cycle of deployments and upgrades, you must follow a structured approach to each match. Use this step-by-step progression guide to secure your loot and build a powerful arsenal.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Your Spawn">
    Immediately upon landing on the surface, open your crafting menu ('Z' by default) and build a **deployable shield**. This provides instant cover for your squad.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select a Contract">
    Open your contracts menu (Caps Lock) and select an active objective. Focus on finding electronic components in ground crates, as they are the easiest to secure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Loot and Mine">
    Explore crash sites and installations to salvage technology, relics, and resources. Collect **biomass** from defeated enemies; this is the only resource that allows you to heal your base health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade at the Warbarge">
    After a successful run, return to your hanger to buy blueprints. Manufacturing weapons from blueprints is much more cost-effective than buying flat-out guns, especially since you lose equipped gear upon death.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Healing Mechanics">
While your kinetic shields automatically regenerate after taking cover for a few seconds, your base health does not. You must hold the heal hotkey (3 by default) to consume collected biomass and restore your health bar.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Extraction Tactics and Risk Management">

Extraction is the ultimate test of survival. The longer you stay planetside, the better the loot becomes—but the environment and hostile players will fight back significantly harder.

| Extraction Phase | Environmental Danger | Tactical Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Early Session** | Low player density, 5 active extraction points | Focus on completing contracts and gathering resources |
| **Mid Session** | Bombardments begin closing extraction points | Keep an eye on the map timer and locate the remaining points |
| **Late Session** | Only 1 extraction point remains active | Expect heavy PvP campouts; coordinate with your squad to push |

<Callout type="danger" title="Orbital Bombardments">
Do not get caught in the red bombardment zones. These orbital strikes systematically destroy extraction points (Harmonic Bridges) one by one and will kill your warclone instantly.
</Callout>

</Section>
