<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapons and Loadout Customization">

Operation Avalon introduces a sophisticated arsenal designed for modularity. Players are no longer restricted to static weapon stats; instead, you can adapt your gear to suit the specific threats of the Lost Convoy. The introduction of the SMG and Shrapnel Cannon provides much-needed variety for close-quarters engagements, while the Laser DMR remains the king of long-range precision.

| Weapon Type | Role | Optimal Range | Key Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **SMG** | CQC Shredder | 0–15m | High fire rate, low recoil |
| **Shrapnel Cannon** | Burst Damage | 0–10m | Devastating against armor |
| **Laser DMR** | Marksman | 40m+ | Precision energy damage |
| **Beam Rifle** | Mid-Range | 15–40m | Sustained energy output |
| **Nova Blade** | Melee | Point Blank | Silent takedowns, no ammo |

<Callout type="success" title="Damage Type Strategy">
Always carry a mix of Kinetic and Energy damage. Use Energy weapons (like the Laser DMR) to strip enemy shields, then switch to Kinetic weapons (like the SMG) to shred their remaining health.
</Callout>

Weapon customization is handled through **Chipsets**. These modular components allow you to modify fire modes, damage types, and handling characteristics. Between deployments, you return to your Warbarge to craft these upgrades using salvaged resources. This progression system ensures that a veteran warclone's weapon is significantly more tailored than a fresh recruit's base rifle.

</Section>

<Section icon={Zap} title="Operation Avalon: New Features and Battlefield Objectives">

Operation Avalon is more than just a playtest; it is a live event that bridges the gap between the ground war and the space war of EVE Online. The centerpiece of this update is the **Lost Convoy**, a battlefield designed to push players toward the "Command Block" at the map's heart.

**Key Battlefield Elements:**
- **Mordu's Legion**: These are not your standard fodder. They use advanced tactics, flanking maneuvers, and heavy ordnance to protect corporate interests.
- **The Command Block**: A high-risk zone tied to the Nemesis storyline. Only the most prepared squads should attempt to breach this area.
- **Resonant Cyphers**: Special event items that can be recovered and traded with empire representatives for exclusive rewards like SKINs and Skill Points.

<Callout type="info" title="Empire Rewards">
Trading Resonant Cyphers in specific empire space (Amarr, Caldari, etc.) supports that faction's secret relationship with the Vanguard. The empire with the most trades will become the primary refuge for Avalon after the event concludes.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning" title="Dynamic Exits">
Be aware that extraction points in Operation Avalon are not permanent. If a zone becomes too "hot" or the mission clock runs low, certain beacons will deactivate, forcing you to cross dangerous territory to reach the final remaining exit.
</Callout>

Successful extraction requires a balance of stealth and aggression. If you are carrying high-value relics, avoid main roads and use the verticality of the Lost Convoy's wreckage to stay out of sight. Use your scanner frequently to detect heat signatures of nearby enemies before they detect you.

<Section icon={Monitor} title="System Requirements and Technical Optimization">

EVE Vanguard is a visually demanding title built on modern architecture. To participate in the Alpha, your PC must meet specific hardware standards. The game requires a 64-bit operating system and a mandatory SSD installation to handle the seamless streaming of the planetary battlefields.

| Component | Minimum Requirement | Recommended Specification |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **OS** | Windows 10/11 64-bit | Windows 10/11 Latest |
| **CPU** | Intel i5-8400 / Ryzen 5 1600X | Intel i5-11600K / Ryzen 5 5600X |
| **GPU** | RTX 2060 / RX 5700 XT | RTX 3080 / RX 6900 XT |
| **RAM** | 16 GB DDR4 | 16 GB DDR4 |
| **Storage** | 50 GB SSD | 50 GB NVMe SSD |
| **DirectX** | Version 12 | Version 12.1 |

<Callout type="tip" title="Performance Tip">
If you experience frame drops during heavy combat, ensure that FSR or DLSS is set to "Quality" or "Balanced" mode. The game is optimized for 60 FPS at 1080p on the minimum specs using these upscaling technologies.
</Callout>

Note that EVE Vanguard utilizes **Easy Anti-Cheat (EAC)** at the kernel level. Ensure your system drivers are up to date and that no conflicting software is running in the background, as the playtest client may refuse to launch if security protocols are breached.
