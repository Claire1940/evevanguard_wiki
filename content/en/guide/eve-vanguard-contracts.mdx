<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Contract Execution">

Executing **EVE Vanguard contracts** requires a disciplined approach to minimize risk while maximizing the output of every expedition. From the moment you launch from your Warbarge to the final seconds before extraction, following a standardized protocol increases your survival rate significantly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pre-Deployment Loadout Selection">
    Before hitting the surface, match your weapon chipsets and suit upgrades to your intended contract. If you are targeting a Mordu's Legion forward base, prioritize kinetic damage and high-capacity magazines.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Planetary Insertion and Recon">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Objective Engagement">

  <Step num="4" title="Loot Consolidation">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies for High-Risk Deployments">

To master **EVE Vanguard contracts**, you must adopt a survivalist mindset. The environment is designed to be oppressive, with smarter Mordu's Legion AI and ruthless human opponents. Success is often determined by the gear you choose to bring and the decisions you make when under pressure.

**Essential Survival Tips:**
- **Manage Your Energy:** Switching between damage types (Energy vs. Kinetic) is vital for bypassing different shield and armor types on elite PvE enemies.
- **Listen for Audio Cues:** The sound of a distant Nova Blade or the hum of a Laser DMR can alert you to nearby players before you see them on your HUD.
- **Use the Environment:** Crash sites often provide verticality. Use high ground to scout objectives before committing to a ground-level assault.
- **Squad Coordination:** If playing in a group, designate roles. Have one player focus on the hacking objective while the others provide 360-degree security.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Always carry a Nova Blade for close-quarters emergencies. It is a silent, hand-forged melee weapon that can finish off wounded enemies without alerting the entire sector to your position.
</Callout>

| **Mobility** | Lightweight Suit Mods | Faster traversal to extraction points |
