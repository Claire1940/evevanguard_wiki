<Section icon={Target} title="How to Extract: Step-by-Step Procedure">

Mastering the technical steps of the extraction process is essential for any Warclone. The UI provides several indicators, but the chaos of combat can easily lead to a missed window. Follow this protocol to ensure a successful return to orbit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate an Active Extraction Zone">
    Open your tactical map and identify green icons representing active extraction points. Note that in **Operation Avalon**, some points are temporary and will deactivate as the planetary situation escalates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage Your Session Timer">
    Every deployment has a hard time limit. You must initiate the extraction process with enough time remaining on the clock to survive the countdown. If the session timer hits zero before you leave, the deployment is considered a failure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear the Landing Perimeter">
    Extraction points are often guarded by **Mordu's Legion** forces or camped by rival players. Before signaling for pickup, perform a 360-degree sweep of the area to ensure you aren't walking into an ambush.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Signal for Departure">
    Interact with the extraction beacon to start the final countdown. This action usually alerts nearby enemies to your location, turning the extraction zone into a "king of the hill" style combat arena.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Defend and Board">
    Stay within the designated zone until the timer reaches zero. Use cover, deployable shields, or suppressive fire to keep threats at bay. Once the ship arrives, you will be automatically extracted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Tactical Timing">
Try to arrive at the extraction zone when there is at least 3-5 minutes left in the total session. This provides a buffer for unexpected firefights or the need to rotate to a secondary exit if the first one is too heavily camped.
</Callout>

</Section>
