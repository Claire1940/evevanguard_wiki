Entering the planetary battlefields of New Eden requires a strong grasp of the core gameplay loop. As an immortal Warclone, death is not permanent, but losing your hard-earned loot is. Follow this step-by-step progression path to maximize your survival rate.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare on the Warbarge">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy Planetside">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure Objectives & Contracts">
    Complete high-stakes contracts. These include raiding corporate forward bases, hacking terminals, and salvaging wreckage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Loot and Adapt">
    Scavenge crash sites for exotic tech and resources. Switch damage types (Energy/Kinetic) to break enemy shields and armor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Extract Safely">
    Locate an active extraction zone. Call for extraction and defend the area until your transport arrives. If you die before extracting, you lose all carried loot.
  </Step>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapons, Loadouts & Customization">

Operation Avalon introduces an expanded arsenal of modular weaponry. Understanding weapon roles and using the chipset system is vital to outmatching rival players.

### Weapon Classification and Roles

| Weapon | Optimal Range | Fire Mode / Damage Type | Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **SMG** | Close | Full-Auto / Kinetic | Fast close-quarters shredder |
| **Shrapnel Cannon** | Close | Spread / Kinetic | High-impact point-blank damage |
| **Beam Rifle** | Mid | Sustained / Energy | Shield-stripping laser weapon |
| **Laser DMR** | Long | Semi-Auto / Energy | Precision long-range rifle |
| **Nova Blade** | Melee | Quick Strike | Lethal close-range finisher |

### Customization and Progression

Weapons can be heavily modified using advanced chipsets prepared on your Warbarge. These modular components allow you to alter firing configurations, switch damage profiles, and customize recoil patterns to suit your tactical needs.

<Callout type="info" title="Adaptive Combat">
Always carry a balance of Kinetic and Energy damage. Use Energy weapons (like the Beam Rifle) to deplete shields, then swap to Kinetic weapons (like the SMG) to quickly tear through physical armor.
</Callout>
