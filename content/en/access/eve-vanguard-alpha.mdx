<Section icon={Target} title="Core Gameplay Loop and Beginner Strategy">

For those new to the **eve vanguard alpha**, the gameplay loop centers on risk management. You deploy as an immortal Warclone, but your gear and loot are very much mortal. If you fall in battle without reaching an extraction point, you lose everything gathered during that deployment.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation at the Warbarge">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Planetary Deployment">
    Upon landing, your first priority is orientation. Check your map for active contracts and the nearest extraction zones. Avoid open fields where you are an easy target for snipers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Looting and Salvaging">
    Search crash sites and corporate installations. Look for advanced technology, relics, and raw resources. These items are essential for crafting and upgrading your gear between runs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Tactical Extraction">
    Extraction points are not permanent. As the session progresses, some exits will close while others become heavily contested. Decide early when to leave to secure your progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapons and Advanced Loadout Systems">

The arsenal in the **eve vanguard alpha** has been expanded for Operation Avalon. Weapons are now more modular, allowing players to switch between damage types, such as energy and kinetic, to bypass specific enemy shields or armor. Understanding the role of each firearm is critical for squad composition.

The introduction of **Chipsets** allows for deep customization. These modules can alter fire modes (switching from burst to full-auto) or add elemental effects to your shots. Since you can carry these configurations between deployments (if you survive), they represent a significant part of your persistent progression.

| Weapon Type | Best Use Case | Key Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **SMG** | Close-quarters combat | Extremely high fire rate |
| **Shrapnel Cannon** | Point-blank defense | High burst damage, wide spread |
| **Laser DMR** | Mid-to-long range precision | Energy-based, high accuracy |
| **Beam Rifle** | Long-range suppression | Continuous damage beam |
| **Nova Blade** | Stealth and finishing moves | Silent melee, ignores light armor |

<Callout type="info" title="Weapon Mechanics">
Remember that energy weapons are generally more effective against shields, while kinetic weapons deal more damage to hull and armor. Coordinate with your team to bring a balanced mix of damage types.

<Section icon={Shield} title="System Requirements and Performance Optimization">

To run the **eve vanguard alpha** effectively, your PC must meet the modern standards of a high-fidelity FPS. The game utilizes advanced lighting and physics engines that require a solid-state drive (SSD) for asset streaming. Running the game on a traditional HDD will likely result in significant stuttering and long load times.

| Component | Minimum Requirement | Recommended Spec |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **OS** | Windows 10/11 64-bit | Windows 11 64-bit |
| **CPU** | Intel i5-8400 / Ryzen 5 1600X | Intel i5-11600K / Ryzen 5 5600X |
| **GPU** | RTX 2060 / RX 5700 XT | RTX 3080 / RX 6900 XT |
| **RAM** | 16 GB DDR4 | 16 GB+ DDR4/DDR5 |
| **Storage** | 50 GB SSD | 50 GB NVMe SSD |
| **DirectX** | Version 12 | Version 12.1 |

<Callout type="warning" title="Anti-Cheat Information">
EVE Vanguard uses **Easy Anti-Cheat (EAC)**. Ensure that no conflicting software is running in the background, as EAC may prevent the game from launching if it detects unauthorized overlays or debugging tools.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="EVE Online Connection and FAQ">

One of the most unique aspects of the **eve vanguard alpha** is its direct connection to the EVE Online universe. Actions taken by Warclones on the surface of planets can trigger events in space for capsuleers. For example, completing enough expeditions during Operation Avalon can generate "AEGIS squalls" in EVE Online, creating new combat and hacking sites for space-faring players.

This "One Universe, One War" philosophy means that even if you prefer ground combat, you are contributing to the grand narrative of New Eden. Rewards like Skill Points, exclusive SKINs, and Resonant Cyphers can be moved between the two games, making Vanguard a valuable tool for any EVE player.
