Getting into the boots of a warclone requires a proper installation. Follow these precise steps to configure the **EVE vanguard launcher** depending on your platform of choice.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Platform">
    Decide whether you want to play natively or through Steam. If you choose native, navigate to the official [EVE Vanguard Website](https://evevanguard.com/) to manage your account.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Native Launcher Installation">
    Download and open the standard EVE Online launcher. Locate the dedicated **Vanguard** tab on the left-side navigation menu. Ensure your client is updated to the latest 2026 build.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Steam Registration Setup">
    Go to the EVE Vanguard Steam store page. Click "Request Access" to join the playtest pool. Once approved, the game will appear as a downloadable title in your Steam Library.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Pre-Download & Verification">
    Whether on Steam or native, download the game files completely before attempting to launch. Verify your game files in the settings menu to prevent deployment crashes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
