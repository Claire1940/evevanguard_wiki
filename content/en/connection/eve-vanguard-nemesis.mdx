<Section icon={Shield} title="The Interconnected Universe: EVE Vanguard & EVE Online">

One of the most ambitious aspects of the **eve vanguard nemesis** event is the direct connection between ground operations and the space economy of EVE Online. Rather than acting as a completely isolated shooter, Vanguard directly influences the ongoing conflicts in New Eden.

As squads of warclones complete faction expeditions on the ground, they contribute Global Points toward a shared community progress gauge. When this gauge fills, it triggers massive events in the space above.

<Callout type="success" title="One Universe, Two Fronts">
Vanguard players do not need to play EVE Online (and vice versa) to enjoy the game, but participating in both fronts provides a highly immersive experience of a shared, living sci-fi universe.
</Callout>

### The Expedition Loop

1. **Complete Ground Expeditions**: Warclones accept contracts from New Eden's factions and the Deathless Circle, deploying to disable Upwell defenses and recover secrets.
2. **Fill the Global Gauge**: Every successful ground assignment adds Global Points to a community-wide tracker.
3. **Spawn Space Convoys**: Filling the gauge triggers the arrival of highly valuable, raidable NPC convoys in EVE Online space (highsec, lowsec, and nullsec).
4. **Capsuleer Interception**: EVE Online capsuleers scan, probe, and intercept these convoys to destroy them and loot Sealed Projects Crates.
5. **Trade for Rewards**: Capsuleers trade these crates at Deathless Wraith Traders to unlock exclusive blueprints, boosters, and cosmetic rewards.
