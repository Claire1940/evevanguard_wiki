EVE Vanguard is not just a standalone shooter; it is the ground-based extension of the massive, decades-old New Eden universe. As an immortal Warclone, your deployments onto hostile planetary surfaces are linked to the ongoing space conflicts of EVE Online capsuleers. Understanding this relationship is critical for players of both titles.

| Feature | EVE Vanguard (Ground War) | EVE Online (Space War) |

This dual-front design means that EVE Online players have a direct interest in supporting Vanguard operations, and vice versa. It creates a symbiotic relationship where alliances can coordinate ground assaults to weaken orbital defenses or secure valuable space lanes.

| Connection Mechanic | Ground Objective (Vanguard) | Space Result (EVE Online) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Global Progress Gauge** | Complete deployment contracts | Spawns raidable convoys in space lanes |
| **Data Hacking** | Hack planetary communication relays | Reveals hidden hacking and salvage sites |
| **Resource Mining** | Extract rare planetary minerals | Feeds materials into high-tier manufacturing |

<Callout type="warning" title="Risk of Loss">

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Operation Avalon: Rewards and Item Traders">

The current Operation Avalon playtest (running July 7–20, 2026) showcases the reward pipeline between the two games. By recovering Vanguard Resonant Cyphers, capsuleers can access exclusive rewards from specialized item traders located across New Eden's major hubs.

### Where to Find Avalon Item Traders
Item traders are stationed in key star systems across empire and low-sec space:
- **Amarr** (Core Empire Hub)
- **New Caldari** (State Trade Hub)
- **Dodixie** (Federation Trade Hub)
- **Rens** (Republic Trade Hub)
- **Sifilar, Kinakka, Ostingele, Hadozeko, Poitot, SL-YBS** (Low-Sec & Null-Sec Outposts)

### Reward Structure and Loot Pool
Exchanging Resonant Cyphers unlocks an extensive array of valuable items, boosters, and cosmetic upgrades:

| Reward Category | Items Available | Impact / Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Equipment & Mods** | Lorharyth's Modified Inertial Stabilizer | High-performance ship agility module |
| **Combat Drones** | Mordu's Legion Aralez Drones | Elite tactical combat and support drones |
| **Character Growth** | Skill Points & Volatile Boosters | Accelerates character training and attributes |
| **Cosmetics** | Vanguard-themed backgrounds & SKINs | Exclusive visual personalization options |
| **Crates** | Arkombine Arisen Reward Crates | Random bundles containing high-value resources |
